Back Home | 3D Cooperative Game

Back Home is the culmination of my undergraduate thesis Wartime Shadow: Using Game Design to Foster Empathy for War’s Psychological Effects. This is a short, narrative-driven game about a girl finding herself by facing her childhood trauma.

Cyberpunk Alley | 3D Environment

As the final requirement of my game art course, we were asked to render any environment or scene of our choice. I decided to go for a cyberpunk aesthetic environment, flush with neon lights and signage, from the view of a dirty, wet alleyway. I used a mix of borrowed textures and self-made materials to decorate my models.

As the final requirement of my game art course, we were asked to render any environment or scene of our choice. I decided to go for a cyberpunk aesthetic environment, flush with neon lights and signage, from the view of a dirty, wet alleyway. I used a mix of borrowed textures and self-made materials to decorate my models. 
<!--more-->
{{< figure src="/images/cyberpunk_render2.png" caption="Up-close angle of a building with a trash can" class="rounded" >}}

As an introductory course to learning Blender, I accomplished all modeling, texturing and rendering tasks within the application. Afterwards, I took the rendered images into Adobe Lightroom to tune their colors. I took heavy inspiration from films like The Matrix, games like Cyberpunk 2077 and Stray, and the work of artists like Helio Frazao. I practice photography as a hobby, so I began this project with some prior experience in composition, as well as photo and video editing. Aditionally, I've seen a *lot* of movies :].
